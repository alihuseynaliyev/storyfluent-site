Privacy Policy

Effective July 4, 2026

No account, no personal data

Storyvo does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email, or any other personal information. We do not run third-party analytics or advertising software of any kind.

Your progress stays on your device

All learning progress — your stories, streaks, and level — is stored locally on your device. We have no servers that store your data, and nothing about your progress is transmitted anywhere.

Speech recognition

Storyvo uses Apple's on-device Speech framework to check your spoken answers. Speech recognition happens on your device, or through Apple's speech services under Apple's own privacy terms. Your voice audio is never sent to Storyvo — because we have no servers to send it to.

Microphone permission

We ask for microphone and speech recognition permission solely to listen to your spoken answers and check them against the story's expected responses. This permission is never used for any other purpose.

Purchases and subscriptions

All purchases, including Storyvo Premium subscriptions, are processed entirely by Apple through your App Store account. We never see or store your payment details.

Children's privacy

Storyvo is not directed at children under the age of 4. Regardless of a user's age, we do not collect any personal data through the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
